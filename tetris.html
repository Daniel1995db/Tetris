Instructions

The rules to this game are simple. You are giving a series of shapes consistently. Your job is to simply stack, group, combine, and build the pieces closely together as possible without leaving many empty spaces.. Each piece drops from the top center of the board; and drops only when the previous piece has collided with another piece or the bottom of the board. When an entire row is filled with pieces, it gets removed from the board and into your Scoreboard. Each row removed from the board is a total of 12 points. Build carefully! because if you don't, the board will fill with pieces and touch the top. As soon as one piece touches the top...GAME OVER!

Use the left and right arrow key to slide your piece left or right. Use the down arrow key to advance its motion downward. Use the Q and W key to rotate your piece left or right.

If you feel the game starts to get to easy, by all means, test your skills and higher the level!
